## Root Cause Findings (Query 03)

- 17,822 total detractor reviews across 24 months
- Top categorized complaint: damaged/defective (16.7%)
- Late delivery: 12.4% of detractors
- Quality below expectations: 14.3%
- Seller unresponsive: 11.2%
- Wrong item: 9.4%
- Uncategorized: 35.9% — signals need for structured complaint capture
- Furniture: 27.2% of complaints are damaged — highest in catalog
- Seller response rate flat at ~35% regardless of complaint type
- Holiday spike: Nov/Dec detractor volume 2x monthly average, consistent both years
- $1.57M GMV tied to detractor reviews total
- Damaged/defective = $284K GMV at risk (highest categorized)

## Late Delivery Findings (Query 04)

- On-time detractor rate: 19.7%
- Late detractor rate: 31.0% — 1.57x lift
- 8+ days late: 44.0% detractor rate — 2.2x baseline
- $329K GMV tied to late-delivery detractor orders
- $1.0M total late GMV — ~1 in 3 late dollars generates a complaint
- Late rate flat at 15-17% for all 24 months — no improvement trend
- Regional hotspots: KY (45.2%), AL (44.3%), AR (43.4%), MS (42.0%), LA (41.3%)
- Pacific NW: OR (41.2%), WA (38.0%)
- CA/TX/IL/NY: 11-12% late rate — 3-4x better than problem states
- Nov/Dec late volume nearly doubles but rate stays consistent (~16-17%)
